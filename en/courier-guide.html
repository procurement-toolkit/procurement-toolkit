Which One to Use, By Situation
Documents / Contracts
Light, time-sensitive documents clear customs quickly, so speed matters most.
DHL / FedEx recommended
Samples (small, low value)
Low-value samples under the de minimis threshold usually clear customs easily, so postal service is often sufficient.
USPS / national postal service recommended
Bulk Cargo (commercial import)
Shipments requiring formal customs entry are safer with a carrier that has strong brokerage support, or a freight forwarder.
DHL/FedEx/UPS + customs broker

Frequently Asked Questions
Which is cheapest — DHL, FedEx, or UPS?

There's no single answer — rates shift depending on origin, destination, weight, and volume for each specific shipment. The most reliable approach is requesting quotes from multiple couriers for the same shipment and comparing directly.

Where can I track an international courier shipment?

Each courier's official website is the most reliable place to track using your tracking number. This page links directly to each courier's official tracking page.
