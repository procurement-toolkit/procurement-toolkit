Global Procurement & Supply Chain Certification Guide

An overview of the most widely recognized procurement and supply chain certifications, and how each one is typically scheduled. Exact dates, fees, and eligibility requirements change over time — always confirm current details on the certifying body's official site before registering.

Note: Most ASCM and ISM exams now use on-demand computer-based testing (available year-round through Pearson VUE) rather than fixed exam dates. CIPS still runs scheduled exam series. This table reflects the general pattern as of 2026 — confirm current windows, fees, and prerequisites directly with each certifying body.
