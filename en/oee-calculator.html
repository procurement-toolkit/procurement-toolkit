Frequently Asked Questions
Is a 100% OEE score actually achievable?

Theoretically yes (no downtime, max speed, zero defects), but in practice it's nearly impossible. Even world-class factories sit around 85%, so tracking improvement over time is more practical than chasing 100%.

Which is easiest to improve: availability, performance, or quality?

There's no fixed answer, but quality improvements often require changing the process itself and take longer, while availability gains from reducing unplanned downtime through preventive maintenance are often achievable more quickly.

How do I determine the ideal cycle time?

Use the manufacturer's rated speed spec, or the average of a measured best-case run. If this number is set too optimistically, your Performance figure will come out lower than it should.
