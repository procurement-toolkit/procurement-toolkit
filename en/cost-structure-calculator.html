Why These Terms Matter

Prime Cost, Manufacturing Cost, and Total Cost cover progressively wider scopes — mixing them up during a cost or margin negotiation can lead to a real pricing error. The same confusion applies to Markup vs Margin: a "30%" markup and a "30%" margin produce different sell prices for the same cost base.

How to use it in practice: If you manufacture, build up from Direct Material and Direct Labor to Manufacturing Overhead and SG&A to see your full Total Cost. If you import/trade, start from Purchase Price and add incidental costs (duty, freight, insurance) to get your Landed/Acquisition Cost before adding SG&A.
