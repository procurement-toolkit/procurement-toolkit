Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

CBM Lab respects your privacy. This is a free tool site for procurement and trade professionals.

1. Data Collection

All calculations run entirely in your browser. Numbers you enter into any calculator are never sent to or stored on a server. We do not require account creation.

We use privacy-respecting analytics (Amplitude, Google Analytics) to understand aggregate traffic patterns (e.g., which tools are used most). This does not include the content you type into calculators.

This site may display Google AdSense advertising in the future. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. You can opt out of personalized advertising via Google Ads Settings.
