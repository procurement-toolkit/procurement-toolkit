    Frequently Asked Questions
    How many days before expiry should the notice period be?

    It varies by contract, but 30, 60, or 90 days is common in practice. Always check the actual renewal/termination clause in the specific contract for the exact period.

    Where is my contract data stored?

    It's stored only in this browser (local storage) and never sent to a server. It won't appear on other devices or browsers, so for anything important, export it as an .ics file and keep a separate backup.

    Do auto-renewing contracts need notice too?

    Yes — arguably even more so. Contracts with auto-renewal clauses often extend automatically on undesirable terms if you don't state your intent to terminate or renegotiate within the notice period.
