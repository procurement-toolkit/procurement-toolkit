Frequently Asked Questions
When should I use an RFI vs an RFP vs an RFQ?

Use an RFI (Request for Information) early on, to gather market intelligence before you've narrowed down suppliers. Use an RFP (Request for Proposal) when you want suppliers to propose a solution approach. Use an RFQ (Request for Quotation) once specifications are fixed and you just need to compare price.

How do I get accurate, comparable quotes back from an RFQ?

Specify quantity, exact specifications, delivery timeline, payment terms, and Incoterm as precisely as possible. Vague requirements lead suppliers to quote against different assumptions, making the quotes impossible to compare apples-to-apples.
